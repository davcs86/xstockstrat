## Summary

Allow multiple users to publish trading strategies on the platform and let others subscribe to automatically copy their trades, creating a social layer with network effects.

## Demotion Rationale (short)

Social/copy trading converts the platform from a personal tool into a regulated financial product. Investment advisor registration, performance marketing rules, and multi-tenant data isolation requirements across every service are not engineering problems — they are legal and regulatory obligations that dominate all other work. Not viable at the current stage.
